Food Service

Our goal is to meet each customer's unique food service needs while maintaining consistent and reliable bulk products. Our food service products are carried in schools, restaurants, and cafeterias across the United States. Whether it's milk, cream, yogurt, sour cream or other dairy products, we deliver the high-quality end products to meet your foodservice needs.

Our food service yogurt is processed using a direct-set method, which allows us to make large quantities. The yogurt is fermented in 32 pound buckets, incubated and chilled. Ordering in bulk is a great way to purchase large amounts for institutional food service, restaurants, catering events, and large group uses. With simpler packaging, ordering wholesale can save the customer money and time especially when using the product as an ingredient to make other food items.

Retail Service

Whether it's producing grocery store/supermarket end products for single serving or on-the-go food items that will be sold in the dairy aisle, Visalia’s retail service can help fulfill and customize products for all your consumer-facing needs.

Ordering for a retail store often involves final product to be sold in the supermarket. These customers often come to us with a single-serving or on-the-go food item to be sold in the grocery store. The supermarket consumer is not buying in bulk, but could be buying for multiple items for a large family or even as little as just one to two persons’.

Co-packing

Visalia Dairy Company offers co-packing, which allows our customers to determine their specifications by using their unique formula, customized packaging and labeling materials. This is an opportunity for customers to have flexibility. Perhaps, you have a processing plant, but got a larger than normal order, we can package the remainder of the order for you. We co-pack anything ranging from 4 gallons to as small as 4 oz. sizes. Feel free to call and we will gladly assist you in filling your additional orders.
