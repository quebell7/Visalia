What We Offer

Food Service

We strive to meet each customer's unique food service needs while maintaining consistent and reliable bulk dairy products. Our food service products are distributed throughout the US - carried in many schools, restaurants, and cafeterias.

Learn More

Retail Service

Whether it's producing grocery store end products for single serving or on-the-go food items or simply packing items that will end up in the dairy aisle, Visalia’s retail service can help fulfill and customize products for all your consumer-facing needs.

Learn More

Co-packing

Visalia is committed to giving our customers flexibility. Our co-packing services can be standardized or tailored to our customers' specifications - accommodating unique formulas, customized packaging and labeling materials.

We are especially proud!

We are especially proud to have teamed up with the Visalia Unified School District and their Vocational Technical Early College (VTEC) High School students to provide them with the opportunity to learn about dairy processing and manufacturing. The students are able to walk away with knowledge about the pilot plant, the dairy manufacturing process and quality assurance tests ran in our lab. This program is designed to encourage local students to successfully enter the dairy industry post-high school or college.
